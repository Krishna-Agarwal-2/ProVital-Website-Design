Lifestyle as medicine: The six pillars.

Nutrition

Evidence supports the use of whole-food, plant-predominant diets to prevent, treat, and reverse chronic diseases.

Physical Activity

Regular physical activity helps manage weight and reduces the risk of chronic disease.

Restorative Sleep

Consistent, quality sleep is essential for cognitive function and physical health.

Stress Management

Effective stress management techniques are crucial for mental well-being and overall health.

Social Connection

Strong social connections are associated with lower risk of chronic diseases and enhanced mental health.

Substance Abuse

Avoiding tobacco, limiting alcohol use, and abstaining from harmful substances are vital for long-term health.
